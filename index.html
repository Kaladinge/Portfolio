About me

Hi, I’m Lars-Inge G. Johnsen, nice to meet you!

I was born and raised just outside of Oslo in 1988. Being a newly educated frontend developer I am looking forward to contribute with my frontend skills, positive attitude and even a joke every now and then.

During the studies I have become proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, CMS(Wordpress, Strapi).

As for the design I like using Photoshop, Illustrator and XD.

I enjoy reading books, gaming and music. The music I enjoy even more if it is progrock, and the most if it is Dream Theater. Also I like learning new things, as witnessed by my educational history, which is one of the most fun things about frontend development.
